Meet Katie Lynn Swiatek

Hello! My name is Katie Lynn and I am current Cast Member at Walt Disney World working towards her M.A. in Web Design and Online Communication. In 2018 I completed my B.A. in Studio Art from Florida State University where I focused on:

  • Typography
  • Web Design
  • Communication
  • Art History

After graduating in 2018, I moved to Orlando, FL to participate in the Disney College Program and have been working there ever since. Within my role in Food and Beverage, I have learned a variety of skills and even been able to apply my design knowledge from my undergraduate program. These skills include:

  • Designing communications materials for use around the location.
  • Becoming an Area Trainer, General Teller, and Relief Coordinator.
  • Improving my customer service skills, as well as team leadership in my Coordinator role.
